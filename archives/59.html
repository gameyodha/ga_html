Grand Theft Auto V released with minor Issue

by Deepak/ April 14, 2015/ No Comments/

Long awaited GTA 5 released today on PC. Its is most graphically polished edition in all of Grand Theft Auto Franchise.

After realised, some of the player who have Windows User Accounts contain certain Characters are having problems installing or playing GTAV. This Issues include an error message saying “The Rockstar update service is unavailable (code 1)”, or the game hanging while trying to download the update. Rockstar responce that they are currently working on this Issue.

Teams says “If you don’t want to wait for a fix, you need to create a new Administrator User Account for your Windows system that only includes letters A to Z, a to z or numbers 0-9 from the basic roman alphabet. For a full list of supported characters, see the table at the bottom of this page. Please note that simply renaming your current User Account will not correct this issue. Once your new account is created (or if you already have an account that only uses these characters) we recommend you restart your PC and log in with that account before starting or continuing with the installation process”.

These are the Supported Character for GTAV PC.
