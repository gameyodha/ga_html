The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

by Deepak/ May 20, 2015/ No Comments/

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is the game that Poland’s studio CD Projekt Red has been making good game for a while now, ever since it debuted this dark fantasy series, based on the books by Andrzej Sapkowski, in 2007.

Wild Hunt is a majestic, earthy open-world adventure with great integrity and personality, this is the best role-playing game in years.

Wild Hunt is the masterpiece of lving, breathing world with great rich and fetile landscape. Sunset is orange with storm clouds, making enviornment more real with lashed winds and rain. Every part of the landcape is detailed.

Geralt of Rivia, a witcher is the main character and freelancue monster slayer with mutant blood who usees silver swords, alchemy and some times magic. Some he is also a ladies man. He wanders town to town on his horse with his own agenda of doing good and helped those who seeks his help. Some times he will get good amount, some times will just do for fun. His life completly surrounded by political unstabily , amy leader for kings and some time his fellow sorcerers friends. Most important his own complicated LOVE LIFE

Combat is much streamlined then previous one and tutorials are good like combat strikes, dodges and counter with gadgets and magical attacks. Postions are great in healing and giving you extra ability but some postions raise you toxicity. Give Geralt more strength with performing upgrade the skill tree or what ever path you choose.

Witcher 3 world is realy huge, it is larger than watch dogs, Assassin’s creed Unity and GTA V.
There are few loading screens like when game starts and ends or selecting some missions. Town feels like actual towns, rather than populated with same models.

Witcher 3 running good with Nvidia Card, while on AMD Card it less pleasent.
Nevertheless game is great from story point and graphics point. I suggest play The Witcher 1 and 2 which give you understanding for Geralt. Some newcomer feels confuse but the bigger picture of game-play is always clear and easy to follow.
